Bayesian Diagnostic Insights

Chatzimichail T, Hatjimihail AT. Bayesian Diagnostic Insights: A Software Tool for Applying Bayes' Theorem in Medical Diagnostics. Ver. 1.2.5. Hellenic Complex Systems Laboratory; 2024.

Software Synopsis

This software tool is a specialized program developed in Wolfram Language for the parametric estimation, visualization, and comparison of Bayesian diagnostic measures and their uncertainty. The program utilizes Bayes' theorem to compute the posterior probabilities for disease and absence of the disease, and the diagnostic threshold-derived positive and negative predictive values. It quantifies their standard sampling, measurement, and combined uncertainty using normal, lognormal, and gamma distributions, applying advanced uncertainty propagation methods. The tool enhances the estimation process and facilitates the comparison of these Bayesian diagnostic measures which are critical for medical practice. Furthermore, it provides a robust framework for analyzing uncertainty, thereby aiding in the understanding and application of probabilistic methods in medical diagnostics.
