Quality Control Using Convolutional Neural Networks Applied to Samples of Very Small Size

RA Chatzimichail, AT Hatjimihail

Abstract

Although there is extensive literature on the application of artificial neural networks in quality control (QC), to monitor the conformity of a process to quality specifications, at least five QC measurements are required, increasing the related cost.
