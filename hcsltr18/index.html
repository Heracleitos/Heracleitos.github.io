Inferences about the Difference between Two Proportions

T Chatzimichail, AT Hatjimihail

Abstract

This Demonstration explores statistical inferences regarding the difference between two proportions of populations with a condition (or trait). It calculates the statistical significance and the confidence intervals for the difference and plots the confidence limits as functions of the selected confidence level. This is done for different population counts with and without the condition, and for different confidence levels used in the estimation of the confidence intervals.
